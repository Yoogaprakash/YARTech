InnovateX Hackathon 2025 is a multidisciplinary event aimed at inspiring college students to develop innovative solutions across a wide range of domains. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their problem-solving skills, collaborate in teams, and work on real-world challenges in cutting-edge fields. This hackathon is designed to foster creativity and collaboration while paving the way for future innovators.
